Hidden Textareas

Purpose: to verify that the edit button does not show up for hidden textareas.

  1. In the boxed area below, there should not be a button or a textarea visible.
     
  2. In the boxed area below, there should not be a button or a textarea visible.
     
  3. In the boxed area below, there should not be a button or a textarea visible.
  4. In the boxed area below, there should not be a button or a textarea visible.
  5. Click on the button to toggle the hiddeness/visibility of the textareas. The edit button should appear within a few seconds. Click the toggle button again, and the edit buttons should disappear in a few seconds. Moving the cursor over a textarea will make the edit button appear quicker.
